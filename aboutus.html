About Us

About IIBT

IIBT Campus, founded in 2023 and based in Akkaraipattu, Eastern Province, Sri Lanka, was established to provide quality education and skill development workshops to students. Our mission is to offer internationally recognized qualifications through a practical and hands-on approach to learning, preparing students for success in their chosen careers.

What We Offer

We specialize in Higher National Diploma (HND) programs across fields such as Quantity Surveying, Logistic Management, and Information Technology, with plans to introduce degree programs in the near future. Our programs are approved by both the Foreign Ministry of Saudi Arabia and the British Council, ensuring global recognition for our graduates.

Expanding Our Reach

While we are physically based in the Eastern Province, we have expanded our services across Sri Lanka through both online and in-person classes. This allows students from all regions to access our quality education and skill development programs, no matter where they are located. Whether you're in Akkaraipattu or elsewhere in the country, IIBT Campus is committed to helping you achieve your educational goals.

Practical, Real-World Learning

At IIBT Campus, we emphasize practical education that bridges the gap between theory and real-world experience. Our students benefit from:

Field visits and site surveying workshops

Industrial training with leading companies

Hands-on experience with real-time projects.

Partnerships for Career Success

To ensure our students are well-prepared for their careers, we collaborate with top universities and leading businesses. Our strong industry ties enable us to offer internship programs and hands-on training, giving students the opportunity to gain real-world experience even before they graduate.

Our Future

Vision

To become a leading educational institution in Sri Lanka that empowers students with globally recognized qualifications, practical skills, and innovative learning experiences, enabling them to excel in a competitive world and contribute positively to society.

Mission

At IIBT Campus, our mission is to deliver high-quality, internationally accredited educational programs through a blend of practical experience and academic excellence. We strive to create an inclusive, student-centered environment that nurtures individual talents, fosters creativity, and promotes lifelong learning. By collaborating with global institutions and industry leaders, we aim to prepare our students for successful careers and leadership roles in their chosen fields, making a lasting impact in both the Eastern Province and beyond.

Goals

Provide Internationally Recognized Education: To offer Higher National Diplomas (HND) and degree programs that meet international standards and are recognized by global accrediting bodies.

Expand Access to Education Nationwide: To extend our reach beyond the Eastern Province through a blend of online and physical classes, ensuring that students across Sri Lanka have access to quality education.

Promote Practical and Experiential Learning: To integrate fieldwork, industrial training, and real-time projects into our curriculum, providing students with the skills they need to excel in the workforce.

Foster Career Success: To build strong partnerships with universities and industries, facilitating internship programs, career counseling, and job placement opportunities that empower our students to achieve professional success.

Contribute to Community and Economic Development: To serve the Akkaraipattu region and beyond by providing educational programs that address local and global workforce demands, supporting the economic and social development of the community.
